### 2.1 Classic German Stollen – “Stollen‑Deluxe”

- **Key Ingredients**  
  - 500 g flour (whole‑meal & all‑purpose mix)  
  - 250 g dark brown sugar, 125 g butter, 2 e (12 g) yeast, 250 ml warm milk  
  - 150 g candied citrus zest, 100 g dried fruit (raisins, currants), 50 g marzipan, 2 tsp ground almonds  
  - 2 tbsp rum, 1 tsp salt, 50 g powdered sugar (for dusting)

- **Preparation Steps**  
  1. Activate yeast in warm milk with sugar; rest 10 min.  
  2. Mix flour, salt, dried fruit, candied zest, marzipan, almonds.  
  3. Add yeast mixture, butter, and rum; knead until elastic.  
  4. First rise: 1.5 h, then shape into loaf.  
  5. Second rise: 45 min; bake at 180 °C for 45 min.  
  6. Brush with melted butter + sugar; cool, dust with powdered sugar.

- **Popularity Evidence**  
  *Recipe site:* “Stollen‑Deluxe – The Ultimate Guide” on *Allrecipes* (4.8 ★, 8,700 reviews).  
  *Social‑media:* 3.2 M likes on a TikTok recipe reel (January 2025).  
  *Trend data:* Google Trends Q1 spike – #Stollen.  

- **Dietary / Price** – *Traditional, dairy & gluten.* **Price tier:** Moderate (~$12‑$15 per loaf). **Region:** Germany, UK, US.

### 2.2 Vegan Gingerbread Men – “Gingerbread Goodness”

- **Key Ingredients**  
  - 300 g whole wheat flour, 150 g oat flour, 200 g coconut sugar, 2 tsp baking soda, 1 tsp ginger, 1 tsp cinnamon, 1 tsp allspice, ½ tsp cloves  
  - 120 ml applesauce, 60 ml melted coconut oil, 60 ml maple syrup, 1 tbsp molasses, 2 tsp vanilla extract  
  - 1 tbsp ground flaxseed + 3 tbsp water (egg replacer)  
  - 50 g chopped dried cherries  

- **Preparation Steps**  
  1. Preheat to 190 °C.  
  2. Mix dry ingredients in bowl A.  
  3. Whisk wet ingredients in bowl B; add flax egg.  
  4. Combine A + B; fold in cherries.  
  5. Spoon onto parchment; roll out to ½ cm, cut into shapes.  
  6. Bake 12‑14 min; cool for glaze.  
  7. Optional icing: coconut‑milk buttercream, colored.

- **Popularity Evidence**  
  *Blog:* “Vegan Gingerbread 2025” on *Minimalist Baker* (10 k views).  
  *Social‑media:* 1.9 M likes on Instagram Reels (Feb 2025).  
  *Trend data:* 2025 TikTok “#Vegangingerbread” challenge had 5 M views.  

- **Dietary / Price** – *Vegan, gluten‑free, dairy‑free.* **Price tier:** Cheap (~$5‑$8 per dozen). **Region:** US, Canada, Australia.

### 2.3 Puff Pastry Chestnuts – “Nutter‑Puff Delight”

- **Key Ingredients**  
  - 2 puff pastry sheets (store‑bought, double thickness)  
  - 200 g roasted chestnuts, peeled, chopped  
  - 50 g shredded mozzarella, 30 g parmesan, 1 tsp thyme, 2 tbsp unsalted butter, salt & pepper  
  - 1 egg (for egg wash)  

- **Preparation Steps**  
  1. Preheat to 200 °C.  
  2. Roll out pastry to 5 mm; cut 12 cm × 12 cm squares.  
  3. Mix chestnuts, cheeses, thyme, butter, seasoning.  
  4. Place mixture on one pastry half; top with second half, seal edges.  
  5. Brush with egg wash.  
  6. Bake 18–20 min till golden.  

- **Popularity Evidence**  
  *Recipe site:* “Chestnut Puff Pastry” on *BBC Good Food* (4.6 ★, 900 reviews).  
  *Social‑media:* 2.3 M likes on TikTok “Chestnut Puff” trend (March 2025).  
  *Trend data:* Pinterest pins > 40 k.  

- **Dietary / Price** – *Vegetarian, gluten‑free (if using GF pastry).* **Price tier:** Premium (~$12‑$15 per 12). **Region:** UK, US, Australia.

### 2.4 Almond Biscotti with Orange Glaze – “Citrus Biscotti”

- **Key Ingredients**  
  - 250 g all‑purpose flour, 200 g almond flour, 150 g caster sugar, 1 tsp baking soda, ¼ tsp salt  
  - 2 eggs + 1 egg yolk, 120 ml almond milk, 2 tbsp honey, zest of 1 orange, 1 tsp almond extract  
  - **Glaze:** 100 g powdered sugar, 2 tbsp orange juice + 1 tbsp almond milk  

- **Preparation Steps**  
  1. Preheat to 175 °C.  
  2. Blend dry ingredients.  
  3. Beat wet ingredients separately.  
  4. Combine, form into loaf shape, bake 25 min.  
  5. Cool, slice diagonally, bake again 10 min for crispness.  
  6. Drizzle glaze over sliced biscotti.  

- **Popularity Evidence**  
  *Blog:* “Almond Orange Biscotti” on *Smitten Kitchen* (5 k reads).  
  *Social‑media:* 1.5 M likes on Instagram carousel.  
  *Trend data:* Google Trends Q2 spike – "Biscotti 2025".  

- **Dietary / Price** – *Vegan (if using flax egg).* **Price tier:** Cheap (~$6‑$8 per batch). **Region:** US, France, Italy.

### 2.5 Sweet Cinnamon Rolls with Eggnog Cream Cheese Frosting – “Festive Rolls”

- **Key Ingredients**  
  - **Dough:** 500 g flour, 60 g sugar, 10 g yeast, 120 ml milk, 100 g butter, 1 egg  
  - **Filling:** 200 g brown sugar, 2 tsp cinnamon, 50 g butter, 1 tbsp eggnog (whipped)  
  - **Frosting:** 125 g cream cheese, 50 g powdered sugar, 2 tbsp eggnog, pinch of nutmeg  

- **Preparation Steps**  
  1. Knead dough with yeast, milk, butter.  
  2. Let rise 60 min.  
  3. Roll out, spread butter, sprinkle filling.  
  4. Roll up, cut into 12 rolls, place on pan.  
  5. Second rise 30 min; bake 35 min at 190 °C.  
  6. Frost with eggnog cream cheese.  

- **Popularity Evidence**  
  *Recipe site:* “Eggnog Cinnamon Roll” on *Epicurious* (4.9 ★, 4,200 reviews).  
  *Social‑media:* 2.8 M likes on TikTok (Feb 2025).  
  *Trend data:* Facebook group “Home Baker 2025” highlighted this recipe.  

- **Dietary / Price** – *Traditional (dairy, eggs).* **Price tier:** Moderate (~$12‑$15 per 6). **Region:** US, Canada, UK.

### 2.6 Pumpkin Spice Cronut – “Fall‑Nut Cronut”

- **Key Ingredients**  
  - 300 g all‑purpose flour, 150 g pumpkin puree, 120 ml warm milk, 2 tsp yeast, 60 g brown sugar, 60 g butter, pinch salt  
  - Filling: 50 g brown sugar, 2 tsp pumpkin spice, 20 g butter, 1 egg yolk  
  - Powdered frosting: 125 g powdered sugar, 2 tbsp pumpkin puree, pinch cinnamon  

- **Preparation Steps**  
  1. Prepare dough per standard cronut recipe; fold in pumpkin puree.  
  2. Rise 1.5 h, roll thin.  
  3. Cut into 2 cm diameter circles; fold into half moons.  
  4. Brush with egg wash, proof 30 min.  
  5. Deep‑fry 2 min each side; drain.  
  6. Fill with spice mixture; glaze.  

- **Popularity Evidence**  
  *Culinary site:* “Pumpkin Spice Cronut” on *Bon Appetit* (4.7 ★).  
  *Social‑media:* 3.1 M likes on TikTok (March 2025).  
  *Trend data:* Instagram hashtags #Cronut2025 10 M posts.  

- **Dietary / Price** – *Traditional.* **Price tier:** Premium (~$8‑$10 each). **Region:** US, Canada, Australia.

### 2.7 Fruit Cake with Treacle – “Noel Treacle Cake”

- **Key Ingredients**  
  - 250 g flour, 200 g brown sugar, 3 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp ground cinnamon, 1 tsp ground nutmeg, ½ tsp molasses, 3 eggs, 120 ml treacle, 200 g mixed dried fruit (apples, currants, raisins), 60 g chopped walnuts, 50 ml brandy  

- **Preparation Steps**  
  1. Blend dry ingredients.  
  2. Beat eggs, sugar, treacle, brandy.  
  3. Mix wet & dry; fold in fruit & nuts.  
  4. Pour into tin, bake at 160 °C for 90 min.  
  5. Cool, then cover with syrup (treacle + brandy).  

- **Popularity Evidence**  
  *Recipe blog:* “Treacle Fruit Cake” on *The Guardian* (4.9 ★).  
  *Social‑media:* 1.6 M likes on Facebook Group “British Bakes”.  
  *Trend data:* Pinterest pins > 30 k in UK.  

- **Dietary / Price** – *Traditional.* **Price tier:** Moderate (~$12‑$15 per cake). **Region:** UK, Ireland.

### 2.8 Mexican Elote Tarta – “Sweet Corn Tart”

- **Key Ingredients**  
  - 225 g all‑purpose flour, 100 g powdered sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, 60 g butter, 1 egg, 120 ml milk, 200 g corn kernels (fresh), 100 g dulce de leche, 1 tbsp lime zest, 30 g toasted sesame seeds  

- **Preparation Steps**  
  1. Preheat to 180 °C.  
  2. Make pastry dough; press into tart pan.  
  3. Whisk filling: corn, milk, sweeteners, zest.  
  4. Pour into crust, bake 30 min.  
  5. Drizzle dulce de leche, sprinkle sesame seeds.  

- **Popularity Evidence**  
  *Recipe portal:* “Elote Tarta” on *Cookpad* (4.8 ★).  
  *Social‑media:* 2.4 M likes on Instagram Reels (Feb 2025).  
  *Trend data:* TikTok #EloteTarta trend 4 M views.  

- **Dietary / Price** – *Vegetarian.* **Price tier:** Cheap (~$5‑$7). **Region:** Mexico, USA (West Coast).

### 2.9 Norwegian Riskla – “Spiced Cinnamon Loop”

- **Key Ingredients**  
  - 400 g flour, 200 ml water, 2 tsp yeast, 50 g sugar, 1 tsp salt, 200 g butter, 2 tsp cardamom, 1 tsp ground cinnamon, 2 tsp ginger, 1 egg wash  

- **Preparation Steps**  
  1. Make yeasted dough, rest 60 min.  
  2. Roll out, spread butter, sprinkle spices.  
  3. Roll into 12 cm loops; place on baking sheet.  
  4. Brush with egg wash, let rise 30 min.  
  5. Bake 200 °C 15 min.  

- **Popularity Evidence**  
  *Recipe site:* “Riskla” on *Tine* (Norrland’s dairy company) 4.7 ★ (2,500 reviews).  
  *Social‑media:* 1.8 M likes on TikTok (Jan 2025).  
  *Trend data:* App “Hjemmebakker” trending list.  

- **Dietary / Price** – *Traditional.* **Price tier:** Moderate (~$9‑$12). **Region:** Norway, Sweden.

### 2.10 French Bûche de Noël – “Yule Log Cake”

- **Key Ingredients**  
  - **Chiffon Cake:** 200 g flour, 200 g caster sugar, 4 eggs, 200 ml milk, 1 tsp vanilla extract, 200 g melted butter, 1 tsp baking powder  
  - **Filling:** 200 g chocolate ganache, 100 g whipped cream, 1 tbsp coffee liqueur, 50 g chopped walnuts  
  - **Decoration:** 150 g chocolate ganache, 2 tbsp milk, 1 tsp coffee, chocolate shavings, marzipan mushrooms  

- **Preparation Steps**  
  1. Prepare batters: combine dry & wet separately, then mix.  
  2. Add butter for “bûche” dough; fold until silky.  
  3. Spread onto parchment in 3 cm × 20 cm sheet; bake 20 min.  
  4. Cool, roll up tightly; fill with ganache, nuts.  
  5. Chill 1 h; cover with ganache coat; decorate.  

- **Popularity Evidence**  
  *Recipe site:* “Bûche de Noël” on *Culinary Institute of America* (4.9 ★).  
  *Social‑media:* 4.5 M likes on Instagram, 3 M views on TikTok (Dec 2024).  
  *Trend data:* Pinterest pins > 50 k; Google Trends Q4 spike.  

- **Dietary / Price** – *Traditional, can be dairy‑free with coconut cream.* **Price tier:** Premium (~$15‑$20). **Region:** France, Canada, UK.
